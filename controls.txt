CTRL+...:
	1   = Black Colour
	2   = White Colour
	3   = Red Colour
	4   = Green Colour
	5   = Blue Colour
	
	Z   = Undo
	Z+Y = Redo
	S   = Save ("Will be saved as 'Untitled.png' in the script directory")
	
(Note that the numbers are not the numpad ones)

E = Eraser
B = Brush
F = Fill Tool
C = Clear Canvas
R = Reset Canvas position

Mouse control:

 - Scroll Up to zoom in
 - Scroll Down to zoom out
 - Hold the middle mouse button while moving the mouse to move the canvas
